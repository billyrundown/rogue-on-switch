# Roguebook

*Roguebook* is a fantasy deckbuilding roguelite from Abrakam. A run begins by choosing two heroes, then develops through card rewards, relics, gems, and positional combat. Its distinctive ink mechanic lets players reveal a procedurally generated hex map one brushstroke at a time.

The Nintendo Switch edition released on April 21, 2022. Nintendo lists a U.S. regular price of $19.99; sale prices are temporary. The included cover image is Nintendo's official storefront artwork.

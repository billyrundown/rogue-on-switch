# Dead Cells

*Dead Cells* is a single-player action roguelite that calls its structure a "Roguevania": runs have permadeath, but paths, mutations, abilities, and weapons can be unlocked for future attempts. The Switch release supports TV, tabletop, and handheld play.

Nintendo lists the Switch release date as August 7, 2018. Standard U.S. MSRP is $24.99; sale pricing changes independently. The local image is from Nintendo's official product artwork.

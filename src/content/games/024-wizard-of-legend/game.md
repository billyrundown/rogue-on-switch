# Wizard of Legend

*Wizard of Legend* is a fast, top-down action roguelite centered on chaining elemental spells. Players assemble a loadout of arcana, relics, and signature spells before tackling the randomized Chaos Trials; it supports solo play or two-player local co-op on one Switch.

The Nintendo Switch edition launched on May 15, 2018. Nintendo lists its U.S. regular price as $15.99. The included cover image is Nintendo's official storefront artwork.

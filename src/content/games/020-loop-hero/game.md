# Loop Hero

*Loop Hero* turns the usual dungeon crawl inside out: rather than directly controlling each fight, players use an expanding deck to place terrain, enemies, and buildings around a hero's looping path. Loot, class choices, and camp upgrades make every expedition a strategic tradeoff between risk and progress. It released on Nintendo Switch on December 9, 2021 for $14.99.

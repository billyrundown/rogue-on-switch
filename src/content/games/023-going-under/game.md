# Going Under

*Going Under* is a single-player action roguelite and satire of startup culture. As intern Jackie, players descend through procedurally arranged offices of failed companies, improvising with breakable workplace objects, temporary skills, and weapons found during each run.

It arrived on Nintendo Switch on September 24, 2020. Nintendo lists a U.S. regular price of $19.99; that price is subject to promotions. The included cover image is Nintendo's official storefront artwork.

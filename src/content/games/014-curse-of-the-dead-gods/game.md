# Curse of the Dead Gods

*Curse of the Dead Gods* is a single-player action roguelike about pushing farther into a hostile, trap-filled temple. Players assemble melee, ranged, and off-hand weapon combinations, then balance the value of powerful relics against a corruption system that adds dangerous curses. It emphasizes deliberate dodges, parries, lighting, and risk management rather than permanent story progression.

The Nintendo Switch edition released on February 23, 2021. Its standard U.S. eShop price is $19.99; check the store for current sales. The locally stored image is official Nintendo Store artwork.

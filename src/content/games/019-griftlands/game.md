# Griftlands

*Griftlands* is a single-player sci-fi deckbuilding roguelite where negotiation can be as important as combat. Its three character campaigns pair different stories and maps with separate battle and negotiation decks, making choices about work, friends, and cards carry real consequences. The Nintendo Switch edition released June 4, 2021; its standard U.S. price is $19.99.

# Monster Train First Class

*Monster Train First Class* gives deckbuilding runs a vertical tactical twist: players protect a pyre across three floors while combining allied clans, minions, spells, and upgrades. This Switch edition includes the base game and *The Last Divinity* DLC. It launched on August 19, 2021 at a $29.99 U.S. price.

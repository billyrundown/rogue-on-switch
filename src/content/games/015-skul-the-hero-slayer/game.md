# Skul: The Hero Slayer

*Skul: The Hero Slayer* is a fast 2D action roguelite that reverses the familiar hero-and-monster setup: the player is Skul, a skeleton trying to rescue the Demon King. Swapping Skul’s head changes his moveset, and the game’s many skulls, items, and quintessences encourage experimental builds across procedurally arranged runs.

The Nintendo Switch edition released on October 21, 2021. Its standard U.S. eShop price is $19.99; check the store for current sales. The locally stored image is official Nintendo Store artwork.

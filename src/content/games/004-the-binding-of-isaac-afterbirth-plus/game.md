# The Binding of Isaac: Afterbirth+

*The Binding of Isaac: Afterbirth+* is a dark, single- and local-multiplayer roguelike built around randomized dungeons, tear-based combat, and extensive item combinations. The Switch launch edition includes the *Afterbirth* and *Afterbirth+* content for *Rebirth*; later *Repentance* content is a separate consideration for the directory.

The game launched with Nintendo Switch in the U.S. on March 3, 2017 (Nintendo's European listing gives September 7, 2017 for that region). Standard U.S. MSRP is $39.99. The local image is Nintendo's official product artwork.
